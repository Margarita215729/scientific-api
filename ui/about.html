About the Artist

Irina Vinokur is a contemporary artist whose work explores the delicate balance between reality and imagination. Born in 1985, she has developed a distinctive style that combines traditional painting techniques with modern digital elements, creating immersive experiences that speak to the human condition.

Her artistic journey began at the Moscow State Academic Art Institute, where she studied classical painting and drawing. After graduating with honors, Irina moved to explore contemporary art forms, eventually developing her signature style that blends ethereal landscapes with geometric abstractions.

Irina's work has been featured in numerous solo and group exhibitions across Europe and North America. Her paintings often explore themes of transformation, memory, and the interconnectedness of all living things. She draws inspiration from nature, urban environments, and the ever-changing dynamics of human emotion.

Artistic Philosophy

"Art is a bridge between the seen and unseen worlds. Through color, form, and texture, I seek to capture moments of transcendence that exist in our everyday experiences. Each piece is an invitation to pause, reflect, and connect with something deeper within ourselves."
